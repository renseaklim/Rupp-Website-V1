EDUCATIONAL PHILOSOPHY

The RUPP university of Cambodia aims to be a leading institution of excellence that aims to develop human capital resources with critical reasoning and ethical decision-making skills, research and innovative skills, well-rounded education in diverse cultural settings, awareness of the potential needs of society and the region, and able students to the workforce or to create jobs and develop themselves to be professionals.
